Fluid images

They key is helping the browser understand the layout. Each image of the set is defined with its size. And the set is defined with its sizes.

<img srcset="image1 image1Size, image2 image2Size, etc..." sizes="[media query] [length], [media query] [length], etc... [default length]"/>

The image size is in w (witch is equivalent to px)

Sizes are defined in px,em or vw

Simple

<img srcset="assets/img/1-large.jpg 800w, assets/img/1-medium.jpg 600w" sizes="100vw" src="assets/img/1-medium.jpg" alt="This guy is cool"/>

With break points

<img srcset="assets/img/1-large.jpg 806w, assets/img/1-medium.jpg 603w" sizes="(min-width:1000px) 20vw, 100vw" src="assets/img/1-medium.jpg" alt="This guy is cool"/>
